Project Description
This Webservice is made for Austrian students to publish and share testcases for programming-tasks. If you participate on a course and you want to share testcases please select your university and click 'Add Testfile'.
If you search for testfiles of a particular course, use the search-box or click on your university and select your course and semster.
Good luck!
